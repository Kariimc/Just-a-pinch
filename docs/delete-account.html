Delete Your Account

You can delete your Just a Pinch account and all associated data at any time. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

1. Open Just a Pinch
2. Tap the Settings tab
3. Scroll to the Account section
4. Tap Delete account
5. Confirm the deletion

Your account and all your recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you no longer have access to the app, email us at kariimchiles@gmail.com with the subject line Account Deletion Request. Include the email address associated with your account. We will delete your account within 7 days.

What data is deleted

  • Your account credentials and profile (name, email)
  • All saved recipes
  • Meal plans and shopping lists

For questions, contact kariimchiles@gmail.com.
