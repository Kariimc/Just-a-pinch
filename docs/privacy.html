Privacy Policy

Just a Pinch · Last updated 12 June 2026

Just a Pinch ("we", "us", the "app") is a recipe organizer that lets you save, organize, and cook recipes. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We aim to collect as little as possible.

Information you provide

  • Account. When you create an account we collect your email address, a password (stored securely by our authentication provider, never in plain text), and the name you enter.
  • Your content. Recipes, meal plans, shopping lists, and profile preferences (such as dietary preferences, household size, and units) that you create in the app.
  • Recipe capture. When you import a recipe, the link you paste, the text you paste, or the photo you scan is processed so we can turn it into a structured recipe.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic technical data required to operate the service (for example, the authenticated session needed to sync your data).
  • We do not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking, and we do not sell your data.

How your information is used

  • To provide the app: store and sync your recipes, plans, and lists across your devices.
  • To capture and generate recipes from links, text, photos, or prompts.
  • To send local reminders you opt into (e.g. a daily meal-plan nudge).
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse.

Third-party services

We share data with the following providers only as needed to deliver a feature:

  • Supabase — hosting, authentication, and database for your account and synced content.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — when you import, scan, paste, or AI-generate a recipe, the relevant link, text, photo, or prompt is sent to Anthropic's API to extract or generate the recipe. It is not used to train their models.
  • Pexels — for recipes without their own image, a dish name may be sent to fetch a stock food photo.
  • Instacart — if you choose to shop your list, your list items may be sent to build an Instacart cart or open the Instacart app.

Data storage and retention

Your content is stored on our servers (Supabase) while your account is active, and a copy is kept on your device so the app works offline. We keep your data until you delete it or delete your account.

Your rights and choices

  • Access & edit. You can view and edit your recipes, profile, and content at any time in the app.
  • Delete your account. Settings → Account → Delete account permanently erases your account and all associated data from our servers and your device. This cannot be undone.
  • Notifications. Reminders are opt-in and can be turned off in Settings or your device settings.

Children's privacy

Just a Pinch is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encryption in transit and row-level access controls so you can only access your own data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
