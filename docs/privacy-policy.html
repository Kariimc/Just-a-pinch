Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Just a Pinch ("the app") is developed and operated by Shift 9 ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

DataWhy
Email addressAccount creation and authentication
First and last namePersonalizing your experience (e.g. your family cookbook)
Recipes, meal plans, shopping listsCore app functionality — your content, stored for you
Crash reports and error logsDiagnosing and fixing bugs (via Sentry)

We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, financial information, or any data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To sync your recipes and data across devices
  • To personalize your in-app experience
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may receive limited data:

  • Supabase — cloud database and authentication provider. Stores your account and recipe data securely.
  • Sentry — crash reporting. Receives error logs and device information when the app crashes.
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI recipe generation and photo OCR. Recipe content you submit for AI processing is sent to Anthropic's API. It is not stored or used to train models.
  • Pexels — stock food photography. Used to fetch a representative photo for recipes that don't have their own image. No personal data is sent.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase servers located in the United States. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Access to your data is protected by row-level security — only you can access your own recipes and account information.

Your recipes are also stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage so the app works offline.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data — including your profile, recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists — is permanently deleted from our servers.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — your recipes and profile are visible in the app at all times
  • Delete your account and all associated data — see instructions below
  • Contact us with any privacy questions or requests

7. Deleting Your Account

You can delete your account at any time from within the app: Settings → Account → Delete account. This permanently and immediately removes your account and all associated data.

Alternatively, email us at kariimchiles@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and we will delete your account within 7 days.

Just a Pinch is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
